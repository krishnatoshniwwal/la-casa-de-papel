#SECURITY LOG : THE B4
#ID : BP-12
#SENSITIVITY : HIGHLY CONFIDENTIAL

B4 (Deep Subterranean Vault Level) — Ultra-Secure Valuables Storage
Access Level: Extreme restriction (higher than B3)
Depth: ~20–30 meters below ground

## Primary Function ## 
Storage of ultra-high-value physical assets:
Diamonds & gemstones
High-value jewelry
Bullion (gold/silver bars)
High-stakes collateral from VIP clients
Rare collectibles (art, watches, etc.)

## STRUCTURAL DESIGN ## 
Reinforced concrete walls (~1–2 meters thick)
Steel alloy inner lining
Blast-resistant design
Seismic isolation supports
Vault Chambers:
--Main central vault hall
--Multiple private vault rooms / safety deposit boxes
Layout:
--1 central corridor
--10–20 vault chambers branching out
--Individual client vault compartments
Vault Doors
--Multi-ton (20–50 ton) vault doors
--Time-lock mechanisms
--Dual-control unlocking (2 authorized personnel required)

## SECURITY LAYERS ##
Physical Isolation
--No direct connection to public areas
--Access only via:
----Dedicated secure elevator (from B3)
----Reinforced stairwell

Multi-Factor Authentication:
To even reach vault zone
--Level 1: Keycard (restricted clearance)
--Level 2: Biometric (iris + fingerprint)
--Level 3: Dual authorization (two-person rule)
--Level 4: Time-based lock windows

Active Monitoring
--24/7 surveillance (controlled from B3)
--Motion sensors + thermal sensors
--Alarm systems

Staffing (Very Limited)
--Vault security officers: 10–15 (rotational)
--Vault custodians: 5–8
--Audit/compliance officers: 3–5
--No unnecessary personnel allowed
