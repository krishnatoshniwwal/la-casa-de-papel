# SECURITY LOG : EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS

## FIRE ALARM
All elevators return to Level 1 and lock.
Stairwells unlock automatically.
Casino partially evacuated.
Guard focus shifts to evacuation management.

## POWER OUTAGE
Backup power activates instantly (no gap in coverage).
Cameras drop to 15 fps during backup power (reduced clarity and tracking speed).
Vault door and B3 locks remain secure (battery-backed).
Sensor systems remain active during outage.

## SECURITY BREACH ALERT
Restricted zones lock down.
All available guards respond to the alert location.
Radio traffic increases significantly.

## EVACUATION
Guests directed to exits by floor staff.
Security focus shifts to guest containment and headcount.
Some internal checkpoints are deprioritised during full evacuation.

## LOOPHOLES
Fire alarm forces stairwell access open — bypasses elevator lockdowns.
Low-power mode (15 fps) reduces camera accuracy and auto-tracking speed.
Multiple simultaneous alerts reduce guard response efficiency.
Guard focus shift during breach leaves other zones temporarily undermanned.
