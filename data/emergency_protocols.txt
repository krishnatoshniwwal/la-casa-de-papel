# SECURITY LOG : EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS
# ID : EL-02
# SENSITIVITY : CONFIDENTIAL

## OVERVIEW ##
Defines system behavior during emergencies.

## FIRE ALARM ##
Trigger:
--Smoke detection or manual activation

Effects:
--Elevators return to Level 1
--Elevators lock
--Stairwells open
--Casino partially evacuated

## POWER OUTAGE ##
Trigger:
--Main grid failure

Effects:
--Backup power activates instantly
--Surveillance switches to 15 fps
--Vault systems remain active

## SECURITY BREACH ##
Trigger:
--Unauthorized access

Effects:
--Restricted zones lock
--Guards converge

## EVACUATION ##
--Guests directed out
--Security focuses on containment

## AI INTERPRETATION ##
Fire:
--Prioritize evacuation

Breach:
--Prioritize containment

## LOOPHOLES ##
--Fire alarm disables elevator usage (forces stair access)
--Low-power mode reduces camera accuracy
--Guard focus shifts away from non-critical zones
--Multiple alerts reduce response efficiency
--Evacuation routes bypass normal security checkpoints
