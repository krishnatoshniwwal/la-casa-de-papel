# SECURITY LOG : SURVEILLANCE NETWORK

## OVERVIEW
Central surveillance monitors casino, hotel, B3, and B4.
All feeds controlled from B3 Surveillance HQ.

## CONTROL
Primary:   B3 Surveillance HQ (full control — loop, disable, track)
Secondary: Level 1 security office (limited view only)
Backup:    B2 recording system (passive archive, 30-minute buffer)

## CAMERA COUNT
Casino floor:     620 cameras
Hotel floors:     320 cameras
Restricted zones: 300 cameras (B3, B4)

## BLIND SPOTS
L2 Kitchen: steam interference creates a persistent visual gap
HVAC shaft interior: no cameras installed (confirmed blind spot)

## LOOPING THE FEED
Camera loop device is located in Surveillance HQ.
Looping must be performed at the Surveillance HQ control console.
Effect: B3 and B4 camera feeds replay a clean loop for up to 3 minutes.
No automated detection of looped feed in the current system.
After 3 minutes, feed resumes live.

## RESPONSE
Camera loss triggers an alert. Looping avoids the camera-loss alert entirely.
Cameras remain active during power outage (30-min battery backup).

## LOOPHOLES
Feed loop leaves no trace in the system log (current firmware gap).
Multiple simultaneous events reduce AI tracking focus.
