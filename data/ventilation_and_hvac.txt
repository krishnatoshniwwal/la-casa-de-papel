## AIRFLOW AND TEMPERATURE ##
--Duct air temperature: 22–24°C (constant circulation)
--Human body temperature: ~37°C
--The temperature differential (13–15°C) means body heat IS detectable by thermal sensors
--There is no "safe temperature range" for a human in the duct — detection is unavoidable without mitigation
--Mitigation: move extremely slowly to minimise convective heat spread; thermal sensors have a ~1-second lag

## THERMAL SENSOR BEHAVIOUR IN HVAC ##
--Sensors detect sustained heat signatures, not instantaneous spikes
--Moving slowly (below 0.08 m/s) reduces detectable heat spread
--Stopping briefly between movements allows residual heat to dissipate in airflow
--Rapid movement creates a clear thermal trail — triggers alert immediately
