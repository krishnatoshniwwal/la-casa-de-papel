# SECURITY LOG : LASER GRID SYSTEM
# ID : TL-01
# SENSITIVITY : HIGHLY CONFIDENTIAL

## OVERVIEW ##
Laser grid systems secure all critical access points including B3 corridors, B3→B4 transitions, and B4 vault perimeter.
System is integrated with central AI monitoring and operates continuously.

## SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE ##
Emitter Nodes:
--Mounted at 1.2m and 2.1m heights
--Redundant nodes every 3 meters
--Independent power microcells (5 min autonomy)

Receiver Nodes:
--Aligned to 0.5 mm precision
--Auto-calibration every 12 minutes

Grid Formation:
--Horizontal beams + vertical beams forming lattice
--Dynamic density adjustment based on alert state

## LASER TYPES ##
Primary Grid:
--Infrared (940nm)
--Invisible to human eye
--Continuous beam

Secondary Grid:
--UV spectrum
--Activates only during alert states
--Denser lattice pattern

## DETECTION PARAMETERS ##
Motion Detection:
--Minimum displacement: 2 mm
--Velocity threshold: 0.1 m/s
--Acceleration threshold: 0.02 m/s²

Thermal Detection:
--Trigger threshold: >30°C
--Safe band: 26–28°C
--Thermal drift tolerance: ±0.5°C

Reflection Detection:
--Detects distortion in beam return
--Tolerance: 3% deviation

## AI INTERPRETATION ##
Movement classified as:
--Natural drift (ignored)
--Suspicious movement (flagged)
--Intrusion (alarm triggered)

AI factors:
--Speed consistency
--Thermal signature stability
--Pattern recognition over time

## ALERT STATES ##
Level 0:
--Normal operation

Level 1:
--Silent alert to B3
--Camera focus increases

Level 2:
--Secondary UV grid activates
--Beam density doubles

Level 3:
--Full lockdown
--Doors seal in 3 seconds
--Security dispatched

## BYPASS MECHANICS ##
Safe Movement:
--Speed: 0.05–0.08 m/s
--Constant velocity required

Body Control:
--No sudden stops or jerks
--Maintain steady posture

Thermal Control:
--Cooling required every 90 seconds
--Heat spikes trigger scan

## FAILURE CONDITIONS ##
--Beam interruption >0.2 sec triggers alert
--Multiple minor disturbances escalate to Level 2

## LOOPHOLES ##
--Emitter recalibration every 12 minutes creates 0.8 sec delay
--Directly below emitters (~2 cm) has reduced detection
--Thermal sensors lag ~1–1.2 sec after rapid cooling
--AI ignores first anomaly if isolated (grace tolerance)
--UV grid activates only after Level 1 alert
--Edge alignment zones near walls have slightly wider spacing (~2–3 cm extra)
