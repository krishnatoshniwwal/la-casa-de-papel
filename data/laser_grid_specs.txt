# SECURITY LOG : LASER GRID SYSTEM
# ID : TL-01
# SENSITIVITY : HIGHLY CONFIDENTIAL

## OVERVIEW ##
Laser grids secure B3 corridors, B3→B4 access, and B4 vault perimeter.
System is always active and monitored from B3.

## SYSTEM STRUCTURE ##
--Emitters at 1.2m and 2.1m
--Spacing: every 3 meters
--Auto-calibration every 12 minutes

Grid Type:
--Infrared (primary, invisible)
--UV grid (activates on alert)

## DETECTION RULES ##
Motion:
--Min displacement: 2 mm
--Max safe speed: 0.08 m/s
--Acceleration spikes trigger alert

Thermal:
--Safe range: 26–28°C
--Trigger: >30°C

## ALERT LEVELS ##
Level 0: Normal
Level 1: Silent alert (no lock)
Level 2: UV grid activated
Level 3: Lockdown (doors close in 3 sec)

## FAILURE CONDITIONS ##
--Beam break >0.2 sec → alert
--Repeated small disturbances escalate level

## LOOPHOLES ##
--0.8 sec gap during 12-min recalibration
--~2 cm gap directly under emitters
--Thermal delay ~1 sec after cooling
--First anomaly ignored if isolated
--Edges near walls have slightly wider gaps
